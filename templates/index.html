    • Purpose

    I always felt that health-care had outdated forms of communication, between different specialities and different providers and between providers and patients as well. This proved as a interesting project for myself. I wanted to work on a project that showcases my skills while tackling a problem many have thought about. The goal here is to allow Patients to have a single point of access for various health-care providers and specialties. Allowing those that frequent doctors to be able to keep track of all communications, prescriptions, and contact information of their many providers. Similary health-care Organizations and providers can gain a Holistic overview of their patients, having access to all of their health-care information. Rendering medical record faxes and secure transfers obsolete. Health-care should be Holistic, thanks for reading!
